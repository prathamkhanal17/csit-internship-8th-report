The internship program at Subisu Cablent Pvt Ltd provided an opportunity to work with Internet Service Provider operations focusing on network monitoring and infrastructure management. This report presents the development and implementation of a network monitoring system designed to enhance real-time visibility of network performance metrics. The primary objectives included optimizing bandwidth utilization, improving incident response times, and streamlining customer support workflows. Over the ten-week period, various technical activities were undertaken including network traffic analysis, router configuration, and development of automated alert mechanisms. The internship experience contributed to understanding of ISP operations, network protocols, and practical implementation of network management tools. This report details the organizational structure, technical projects, weekly activities, and learning outcomes achieved during the internship tenure at the Network Operations Center in Chitwan, Nepal.
