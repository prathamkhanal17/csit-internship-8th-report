\chapter{Work Logs}

This appendix presents the chronological record of activities undertaken
during the ten-week internship period at the Subisu Cablenet Ltd. These
weekly logs detail the day-to-day responsibilities, technical tasks, and
troubleshooting procedures performed under the supervision of the senior
\textbf{Mr. Biplab Panta}. They serve as an official verification of
attendance and provide a granular view of the progression from theoretical
orientation to hands-on network configuration and system administration.
